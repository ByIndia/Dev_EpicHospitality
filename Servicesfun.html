Our Services

Epic Hospitality services Private Limited, its main core business is Food Catering and Housekeeping services for Corporates,Institutes, Industries, Event Management, Indoor and Outdoor catering.

Epic Hospitality owns their food production center by name,Central Kitchen, at Putlur, Tiruvallur District, Chennai.

Epic Hospitality, is also registered with Karnataka State Government for e-procurement in attending government tenders.
